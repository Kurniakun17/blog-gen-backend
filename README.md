## Beyond the sticker price: The real cost of Chatbase

The official pricing page really only gives you part of the picture. To figure out what you'll actually spend on Chatbase, you have to look at how its credit system really works and account for the cost of add-ons that you'll probably need.

### The confusing message credit system

This is where things get complicated. You see \"message credits\" and think one credit equals one chatbot response. That would make sense, but it's not how it works. The number of credits a single response uses up depends entirely on which AI model you choose.

For instance, a high-quality model like GPT-4 can [burn through 20 credits](https://chatimize.com/reviews/chatbase/), according to Chatbase's own docs. A weaker model might only use one credit. This system creates a huge headache: your costs become totally unpredictable. If you’re on the Standard plan with 10,000 credits, you might feel set for 10,000 customer chats. In reality, if you need a smart AI model to give good answers, you could blow through your whole monthly budget after just 500 conversations.
